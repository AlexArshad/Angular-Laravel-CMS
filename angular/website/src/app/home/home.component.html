    Our Vision

    In today’s world, where artistic and creative forms of mass communication is dominating the entire media space, our vision is to provide a platform to our students which can ensure top notch and world-class standard of media and creative studies.

    Our Aim

    Our aim is to emerge as the pioneer of media education and training in Nagpur without any social or cultural barrier. We aim to bring in young, talented students and train them to be excellent media personnels, budding entrepreneurs and change makers.

    What we offer

    Celebrated names: Well-known names from the media, film, television, business and social verticals of the society, visit our institute as guest speakers/moderators and guide. They not only share their experience but also help in channelizing students towards the verticals where they can make the most amount of progress through one-on-one interactions.

    Rich, enriching workshop with the working professionals: With a heavy line-up of our well-qualified alumni, who are now renowned names in the industry, we make sure that each one of our student gets a personalised real-time interaction with our experts. Our interactive workshops ensure rich, enlightening sessions with the crème de la crème of the media industry.

    Discussion/ Feedback:

    With the number of student admissions restricted in each batch, we focus on each one of our students, completely. We believe that the next generation of professionals can only reach the pinnacle of success through constant and continuous discussions/ projects/ assignments/ reviews and feedback.

    State-of-the-art technology: With its cutting edge technological advances, latest softwares, high quality studios and excellent teaching methods, Art Pickle, has emerged as one of the leading media institutes in Pune and commits to reach the same level of expertise in Nagpur too.

    Refreshing learning curve: With our dynamic and interactive teaching process, we provide a refreshing learning experience to our students. Less of theoretical and more of on-the-field classes, enables our students get a real-time experience and understanding of what are the challenges, expectations and demands of a being working media professional.
